Corporate Responsibility

At STS, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of corporate responsibility. This includes upholding ethical practices and ensuring that our operations are conducted with integrity and respect for human rights.

Commitment to Ethical Practices

We believe in conducting our business in an ethical manner. This includes treating all individuals with respect, providing fair labor conditions, and ensuring that our supply chain is free from exploitation and abuse.

Modern Slavery Act 2018

STS is fully committed to complying with the Modern Slavery Act 2018. This legislation requires companies to ensure that there is no modern slavery or human trafficking in their business operations or supply chains. Modern slavery encompasses slavery, servitude, forced or compulsory labor, and human trafficking. We take a zero-tolerance approach to modern slavery and are committed to acting ethically and with integrity in all our business dealings and relationships.

Our Policies and Practices

We have implemented policies and practices to identify and mitigate the risk of modern slavery within our organization and supply chains. These include conducting due diligence on suppliers, requiring them to adhere to our Supplier Code of Conduct, and ensuring that our procurement processes are transparent and fair. We also provide training to our employees and partners on the importance of ethical practices and the identification of modern slavery risks.

Monitoring and Reporting

We continuously monitor our operations and supply chains to ensure compliance with the Modern Slavery Act 2018. We encourage all employees, partners, and suppliers to report any concerns regarding potential modern slavery issues. Reports can be made confidentially and without fear of retaliation.

Community Engagement and Social Responsibility

In addition to our commitment to ethical business practices, STS actively engages with the communities in which we operate. We support initiatives that promote education, health, and well-being, and we strive to make a positive impact through our corporate social responsibility programs.

Continuous Improvement

We are dedicated to continuously improving our corporate responsibility practices. We regularly review and update our policies and procedures to ensure they reflect the latest standards and best practices. We welcome feedback from our stakeholders and are committed to transparency in our operations.

Thank you for your interest in STS's corporate responsibility efforts. We believe that ethical business practices are not only the right thing to do but also essential for building a sustainable and successful business.
